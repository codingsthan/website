About SGES

SGES is a leading environmental consultancy organization offering expert services like Environment Clearance, Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA), Compliance Monitoring, CTE & CTO Approvals, CGWA NOC, Mining Plan Development, Drone Surveys, and GIS Mapping. We are committed to delivering sustainable and regulatory-compliant solutions that help businesses thrive while protecting our environment.

Our Services

Environmental Clearance (EC)

We provide complete Environmental Clearance services including baseline studies, EIA/EMP report preparation, public hearing coordination, and expert handling of MoEF&CC/SEIAA approvals for mining, construction, and industrial projects.

Consent to Establish / Operate

We assist industries, infrastructure, and construction projects in securing Consent to Establish (CTE) and Consent to Operate (CTO) from State Pollution Control Boards, including complete documentation, pollution control planning, and compliance management.

CGWA Clearance

Our team provides end-to-end support for obtaining CGWA approvals by conducting hydrogeological surveys, preparing impact assessments, and ensuring compliance with all groundwater extraction regulations and norms.

Wildlife & Forest Clearance

Expert facilitation for obtaining Forest and Wildlife Clearances, including ecological assessments, vegetation studies, forest land diversion documentation, and liaison with Forest and Wildlife Departments at all administrative levels.

GIS & Remote Sensing

Advanced GIS and Remote Sensing services for spatial analysis, land use mapping, environmental impact studies, and project feasibility reports to support precise and efficient decision-making.

Drone Survey

High-resolution aerial surveys using drone technology for detailed topographic mapping, volumetric analysis, environmental monitoring, and infrastructure planning even in challenging terrains.

Core Drilling

Precision core drilling services for geotechnical investigations, mineral exploration, and soil sampling, ensuring high-quality sample recovery essential for project planning and feasibility studies.

DGPS Survey

Conducting highly accurate DGPS surveys for lease boundary demarcation, mining project mapping, compliance layouts, and legal land record preparation with the latest GPS technology.

Conservation Plan

Preparation of integrated Conservation Plans for flora, fauna, and habitats affected by developmental projects, ensuring alignment with Forest and Wildlife Department guidelines for biodiversity protection.

Exploration

Comprehensive geological exploration services including mapping, geophysical surveys, sampling, and reserve estimation, assisting clients from project initiation to resource development stages.

Environmental Compliance

We ensure full environmental compliance for industries by providing timely audits, environmental monitoring, and fulfillment of all EC/CTO and post-clearance conditions efficiently.

Environmental Audit

Systematic environmental auditing services aimed at identifying gaps, enhancing sustainability initiatives, ensuring legal compliance, and promoting eco-friendly operational practices.
